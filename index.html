Frequently asked questions

How does Ovrin handle calls that come in after hours or when my team misses them?

Ovrin can answer, capture the issue, collect contact details, and trigger the next step so the lead does not sit untouched.

Will Ovrin sound robotic or natural to homeowners?

The goal is a calm, useful, service-business experience. It should feel like a helpful front desk layer, not a gimmick.

What if we already use a scheduler, CSR team, or HVAC software?

Ovrin is meant to support your existing workflow, not replace everything. The audit identifies where the current process leaks before suggesting changes.

How does Ovrin decide when to book, text, or hand the lead to a human?

The workflow can be designed around urgency, job type, service area, business hours, and your preferred booking rules.

How long does setup take and what do you need from us?

The audit starts with your current lead flow, website or Google Business Profile, call handling process, and follow-up process.

Can Ovrin work if our team still wants final control over bookings?

Yes. The system can capture and qualify leads, then route them to your team for final confirmation.

What happens if someone calls with an emergency or something the system is unsure about?

The system should escalate, flag, or route unclear cases based on your rules instead of pretending to know everything.
