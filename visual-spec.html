Objective

Restart Ovrin as a simple, functional no-video lander.

Make the first screen explain the leak, show that Ovrin recovers demand, and push HVAC owners toward a leak audit without relying on a media player section.

Success Criteria

No Video

No fake player, media section, or video tracking code.

Functional

Static HTML/CSS/JS, existing audit form route, build/check pass.

Editable

Design system lives in DESIGN.md; variants live in prototype.html.

Brand Aligned

Black ink, white space, Ovrin green, copper/water only as metaphor.
